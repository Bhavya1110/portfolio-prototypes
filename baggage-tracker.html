Lost Baggage Tracker

Problem: Users have no visibility into where their luggage is during transit, leading to anxiety and frequent customer service calls.

Solution: Real-time baggage tracking with visual progress indicators and proactive notifications.

Key Features:

• 3-step tracking flow from check-in to baggage claim

• Real-time location updates with airport visualization

• Proactive notifications at each checkpoint

• Reduced dependency on customer service
